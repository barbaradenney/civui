`<civ-support-resources>`

A structured container for support and crisis contact information. Renders as an `<aside>` landmark with a heading and slotted content for phone, email, and other contact links. Use at the bottom of sensitive forms — benefits, healthcare, housing — to provide crisis lines, helpdesk numbers, or support links.

## Slots

The default slot accepts any content — typically `civ-action-link` components for phone/email:

```html
<civ-support-resources heading="If you need support">
  <civ-action-link type="phone" number="988" label="Veterans Crisis Line (press 1)"></civ-action-link>
  <civ-action-link type="phone" number="18774243838" label="Homeless Veterans Hotline"></civ-action-link>
  <civ-action-link type="email" address="help@va.gov" label="Email VA support"></civ-action-link>
</civ-support-resources>
```

You can also include paragraphs, lists, or any custom HTML:

```html
<civ-support-resources heading="Housing assistance">
  <p>If you are experiencing homelessness or at risk of losing your housing:</p>
  <civ-action-link type="phone" number="18774243838" label="National Call Center for Homeless Veterans"></civ-action-link>
  <p class="civ-mt-2 civ-text-sm">Available 24/7.</p>
</civ-support-resources>
```

## Accessibility

- Renders as `<aside role="complementary">` — a landmark for screen reader navigation
- Heading uses `aria-labelledby` to label the landmark (no duplicate announcements)
- Heading level is configurable to fit the page's heading hierarchy
- Always visible — not hidden behind conditionals or collapsed sections
